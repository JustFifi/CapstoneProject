About Us

Welcome to StreamReviews, your number one source for stream reviews. We’re dedicated to giving you the very best reviews, with a focus on streamers from Twitch.tv

Founded in 2015 by Fifi, StreamReviews has come a long way from its beginnings as a college capstone project. When Fifi first started out, his passion for streaming drove him to choose this idea as his graduation project, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming resource for streamers alike.

We hope you enjoy our site as much as we enjoy offering this to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
